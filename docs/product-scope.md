# Product Scope

Phase 1 launches Payn as a trust-first European financial marketplace covering loans, credit cards, money transfers, and currency exchange.

## Phase progression

- Phase 1: affiliate redirects, editorial comparison, SEO-led discovery.
- Phase 2: lead capture, saved preferences, and intent capture.
- Phase 3: broker routing, embedded partner journeys, and personalized recommendations.

## Core capabilities

- Category and country-aware catalog retrieval
- Configurable ranking
- Provider profiles and trust content
- Affiliate click tracking
- Lead capture readiness
- Future admin and ingestion workflows
